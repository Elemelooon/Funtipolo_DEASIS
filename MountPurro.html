Mount Purro Nature Reserve

Mount Purro Nature Reserve is a serene and eco-friendly haven nestled in the heart of Calawis, Antipolo, Philippines. This nature reserve offers a refreshing escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, providing visitors with a chance to immerse themselves in the beauty of the natural world.

One of the primary features that make Mount Purro Nature Reserve stand out is its commitment to sustainability and conservation. The establishment prides itself on being an eco-friendly destination, promoting responsible tourism and environmental awareness. The reserve is dedicated to protecting its lush surroundings, including rainforests, diverse wildlife, and crystal-clear streams.

Visitors can engage in a variety of activities that allow them to connect with nature and learn about its preservation. From nature walks and hiking trails to birdwatching and wildlife encounters, the reserve offers numerous opportunities to appreciate the biodiversity and wonders of the Philippine ecosystem.

The accommodation at Mount Purro Nature Reserve is also noteworthy, with charming bamboo cabins and huts that blend harmoniously with the natural environment. Staying in these rustic yet comfortable lodgings provides guests with a unique experience, waking up to the sounds of chirping birds and rustling leaves.

Beyond its environmental efforts, Mount Purro Nature Reserve has a strong focus on community development and sustainability. The establishment actively involves the local community, providing employment and empowering the residents to participate in conservation efforts and support eco-tourism initiatives.

Overall, Mount Purro Nature Reserve offers a peaceful and educational retreat, combining environmental consciousness with genuine Filipino hospitality. It's a fantastic destination for eco-conscious travelers and nature enthusiasts seeking a mindful and immersive experience within the lush landscapes of the Philippines.

Note that you need to make prior reservation before your visit. No walk-ins allowed.

Office Hours:
9:00AM-5:00PM (Monday-Friday)
Entrance Fee:
Admission rate varies depending on the activities and inclusions, but it ranges from P300 to P750 for children and from P450 to P950 for adults. You may check the official website for the complete list of packages.
Location:
Purok 5, Barangay Calawis, Antipolo, Rizal
