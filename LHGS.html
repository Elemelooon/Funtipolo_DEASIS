Luljetta's Hanging Gardens Spa

If you’re looking for a quick getaway from Manila but don’t want to go too far, there’s a little resort in Antipolo City that you can visit! Luljetta’s Hanging Gardens is a charming property known for its spa treatments and relaxing facilities that are sure to give you the pampering that you need. You can book any of their services via Klook and gain access to their amazing amenities including a sauna, heated jacuzzi, a hydro-massage pool, and infinity pools that can give your body the rest it deserves. If you want to level up your experience, you can also try their massages and body scrubs for the ultimate spa day! If you’re feeling hungry after enjoying Luljetta’s facilities, you can also opt to devour a delectable meal before you leave. Just make sure to bring a friend or loved one along and share this experience together!

Luljetta's Hanging Gardens Spa is a renowned wellness retreat located in Antipolo, Philippines. Nestled in the scenic mountains of Rizal province, the spa offers a serene and tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. It is famous for its stunning hanging gardens, which provide a beautiful and calming atmosphere for visitors to unwind and rejuvenate.

Luljetta's Hanging Gardens Spa provides a wide range of spa treatments and therapies, including massages, facials, body scrubs, and various hydrotherapy treatments. Guests can also indulge in relaxation pools with breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

One of the highlights of the spa is its infinity pool that seemingly blends into the horizon, creating a unique and magical experience. The spa's location atop the mountains offers picturesque vistas of the metro Manila skyline and the lush natural surroundings, making it a popular destination for both locals and tourists seeking a memorable and relaxing escape.
