Cloud 9

Cloud 9 in Antipolo is a popular tourist destination and a stunning viewpoint located in the province of Rizal, Philippines. It is known for its breathtaking panoramic views of the Manila skyline, the surrounding mountain ranges, and the lush green landscapes below.

To reach Cloud 9, visitors often need to trek or take a short hike up a hill. Once at the top, they are rewarded with a mesmerizing view that makes the effort worthwhile. It's an ideal spot for photography, picnics, or simply relaxing in the fresh mountain air while taking in the scenic beauty.

Cloud 9 Antipolo is one of the most visited places in Rizal province which is located on the Sumulong Highway in Antipolo City. The hanging bridge is one of the main attractions in the event venue and it’s one of the most visited hanging bridges in Rizal. From the viewing deck or from the top of the hanging bridge, it offers tourists a panoramic 360-degree view of Antipolo City, the Sierra Madre mountain range as well as the entire Metro Manila skyline. On a clear afternoon, visitors can have an impressive view of the sunset.

Cloud 9 Antipolo is just one hour and thirty-minute drive from the National Capital Region. It is roughly twenty kilometers away from the east of Metro Manila. For this reason, it is one of the most popular day tour trips near Metro Manila. In addition, it is only a fifteen-minute drive from downtown Antipolo City.

According to its past history, Cloud 9 Antipolo was opened to the public in the year 1995 as a humble restaurant that serves lavish plates to customers. It is formerly known to the locals as the Cafe Nueve. The owner of Cloud 9 Antipolo is Doctor Potenciano Malvar and Mrs. Lourdes C. The owners realized the development of the mountainside that visitors coming from different parts of Luzon are eager to visit just for the confounding view. For this reason, Cloud 9 Antipolo gradually grew as a major tourist attraction. As the years passed by, the restaurant incorporates accommodation and recreation establishments. For the past seventeen years, it became a hotel and restaurant business.

As of today, Cloud 9 Antipolo is also known as the Cloud 9 Sports and Leisure Club. It is the towering point of Antipolo City. It has an art gallery, seventy-eight rooms, two swimming pools, and seven different venue halls. Also, it is home to the most sizeable mural painting in Antipolo City created by Mr. Othoniel Neri which has 4 meters in height and 9 meters in length.

Cloud 9 is the name of the hotel restaurant event venue because from the terrace you will feel like you’re in heaven already. The operating hours at Cloud 9 Antipolo is every day from 8 am until 10 pm in the evening. So if you are looking for a perfect place to relax and chill then this is the place to go. It should be included in your travel bucket list. All in all, I advocate you to visit this hotel and restaurant as soon as possible to experience a cool ambiance and appetizing food.

Opening Hours:
8:00AM-10:00PM (Monday-Sunday). This schedule is for the restaurant, 360° View Deck, and hanging bridge. The opening hours might change without prior notice, so please check the official website or Facebook page before visiting.
Entrance Fee:
P60: 360° View Deck + Hanging Bridge
FREE, if you are eating at Cloud 9 Restaurant. If the total cost of your order is above P300 per person, you’ll get complimentary admission to the hanging bridge, 360° View Deck, and Butuan Caraga Heritage Museum. You need to present the receipt, so keep it with you.
Location:
Sumulong Highway, Brgy. Sta Cruz, Antipolo City
